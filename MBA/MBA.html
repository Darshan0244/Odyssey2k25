The Ultimate Logo Quiz

Logo Quiz Event Rules

  • Round 1 (Preliminary)
  • 45 logos – 45 minutes
  • Write company names on answer sheets.
  • ✅ Correct: +5 points
  • ❌ Wrong: -5 points
  • Top 4 teams move to Round 2.
  • Round 2 (Final)
  • 50 logos – 45 minutes
  • Same rules as Round 1.
  • Tie-Breaker (if needed)
  • 5 logos (30 sec each).
  • Buzzer round: First to hit guesses.
  • Wrong answer: -5 points.
  • Highest score wins.

The BlindFold Challenge

The BlindFold Challenge Event Rules

  • Game Overview: 2 players; one blindfolded, the other guiding.
  • The blindfolded player navigates a minefield with obstacles (mines).
  • The guiding player gives verbal instructions but cannot touch the blindfolded player.
  • The blindfolded player starts at the designated point and must safely reach the finish line.
  • If the blindfolded player steps on a mine, they must return to the start.
  • The blindfolded player wins by reaching the finish line without touching a mine.
  • Time Limit: 2 minutes.

Balance The Ball

Balance The Ball Event Rules

  • The participants will be given Table Tennis Ball and Table Tennis Bats.
  • They have to balance the ball with the help of a bat and basket it within 1 minute.
  • The ball should not touch the ground, if it happens the team will lose the game.
  • Participation – Minimum 2 Members.
  • Time Limit for the game: 1 minute.
