Department of Electronics and Communication Engineering

Technical Events

Robo Roadies (Robo Race)

Speed, precision, innovation, strategy, adrenaline, competition, challenge, control, victory, dominance!

  • The fastest and most balanced robot wins.
  • Robots must meet the given specifications.
  • Teams can have up to two members.
  • All members must carry their institute ID.

Non-Technical Events

Treasure Hunt Saga

Exciting quest solving clues, exploring locations, uncovering hidden treasures.

  • Team members may/may not be changed during the event.
  • Clues must be found and solved in order.
  • The hunt must be completed within a time limit.
  • Certain areas may be off-limits.

BGMI

Thrilling battle royale, strategic gameplay, intense survival, ultimate victory mission.

  • Cheating, hacking, and third-party software are prohibited.
  • Players must use their own accounts.
  • Mobile-only; no college internet access.
  • Unsportsman like conduct is not allowed.

5-a-side Football

Fast-paced game, quick reflexes, teamwork, goal-scoring, intense, fun, strategy.

  • Teams have five players: four outfield and one goalkeeper.
  • The pitch is smaller than a standard football field.
  • Goalkeepers have restricted actions in their area.
  • Sliding tackles are usually prohibited or heavily penalized.
